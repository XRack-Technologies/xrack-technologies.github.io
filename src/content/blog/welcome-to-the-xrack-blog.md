This is where the team writes down how XRack is built and where it is going. Expect a few kinds of
posts: **engineering** deep-dives into the internals, **research** on how we measure and improve the
harness, **field notes** from running agents in the real world, and the occasional piece on where we
think agentic AI is headed.

## How it's organized

Every post is filed under a category you can reach from the navigation. Whatever you are here for —
evaluating, integrating, or just following along — there should be a lane for it.

More soon. In the meantime, the product itself lives at [xrack.io](https://xrack.io).
