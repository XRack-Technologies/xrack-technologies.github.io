# XRack Blog — Growth & Readiness Audit

### AUD-C3 — No email capture / newsletter (no owned audience)
- **Area:** Community / marketing
- **Problem:** There is no subscribe form, newsletter, or any way for a reader to opt in. RSS exists but is not a substitute for an owned email list.
- **Impact:** Traffic from PR/launches leaves without a way to be re-reached. No owned channel for announcements, nurturing, or community growth; the highest-leverage growth asset is missing.
- **Acceptance criteria:** subscribe form wired to an ESP (double opt-in, confirmation, list/segment); subscribe CTA on the homepage and at the end of each post; success/error states; unsubscribe handled by the ESP.
- **Effort:** M
